DEAN AMBROSE



About

Dean ambrose is a WWE superstar. His signature move is dirty deeds. Known for his unpredictability. He runs a show called Ambrose Asylum. He was also part of the shield with Roman Reigns and seth Rollins. He has won WWe World Heavy Weight Championship, Tag Team Championship and United States Championship. He has left WWe in 2019 and joined AWE under the ring name as Jon Moxley.
